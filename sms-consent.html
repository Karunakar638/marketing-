SMS Consent Policy – Nexus Media Group

Nexus Media Group sends SMS messages only to users who provide clear permission (opt-in) to receive text messages from us.

How users give consent

  • Users voluntarily text our toll-free number after hearing our voicemail or viewing our marketing materials or website.
  • The first inbound message from the user (for example: “Hi”, “Info”, “Yes”, or any other message) is treated as explicit opt-in consent to receive SMS related to their inquiry.
  • We do not send unsolicited, cold, or promotional text messages to individuals who have not initiated contact with us.

User controls

  • Users can reply STOP at any time to unsubscribe from SMS messages. After sending STOP, they will no longer receive SMS from us, except a final confirmation message.
  • Users can reply HELP for assistance or more information about our SMS program.

Message content and frequency

Messages are related to the user’s request, property, or marketing services provided by Nexus Media Group. Message frequency may vary based on ongoing communication, but only users who have opted in will receive SMS.

Our compliance

We follow Twilio, CTIA, and TCPA guidelines for SMS opt-in, messaging, and unsubscribe behavior. We do not sell or share phone numbers for third-party marketing.
